## BUTTON COLORS & STATES

### PRIMARY (Red/Orange Gradient)
- **Use**: Main actions (Add to Cart, Buy Now, Place Order)
- **Hover**: Slightly darker, lifted effect
- **Active**: Slightly darker, pressed effect

### SECONDARY (Light Gray)
- **Use**: Alternative actions (Cancel, Close)
- **Hover**: Darker gray
- **Active**: Even darker

### NAVIGATION (Dark Blue)
- **Use**: Menu items, navigation
- **Hover**: Color change
- **Active**: Gold underline

### ICON (Mixed)
- **Use**: Quick actions (Add, Remove, Cart)
- **Hover**: Scale up slightly
- **Active**: Scale down

## TYPICAL ACTIONS

### Browse Products
1. Search or click Category
2. Click Product Card
3. Read Details
4. Adjust Quantity
5. Click Add/Buy

### Add to Cart
1. Click Product
2. Adjust Quantity (optional)
3. Click Add to Cart
4. Notification appears
5. Continue shopping or view cart

### Checkout
1. Click Cart Button
2. Review items
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Fill address
5. Choose payment method
6. Enter payment details
7. Click "Place Order"
8. See confirmation

### Login/Register
1. Click "Hello, sign in"
2. Click Login or Register tab
3. Fill form
4. Click Sign In/Create Account
5. Success notification
6. Modal closes

## BUTTON STATES EXPLAINED

### ENABLED (Ready to Click)
```
Background: Full color
Cursor: Pointer
Hover: Visible effect
```

### DISABLED (Can't Click)
```
Background: Faded
Cursor: Not-allowed
Hover: No effect
```

### LOADING (Processing)
```
Background: Faded
Cursor: Wait
Shows: Spinner or text
```

### ACTIVE (Currently On)
```
Background: Darker
Border: Underline
Cursor: Pointer
```
