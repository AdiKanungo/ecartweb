## 💾 How Data is Saved

Everything works **locally on your browser**:
- User accounts stored locally
- Shopping cart persists
- Orders saved
- Data survives browser close
- No internet needed after loading

### View Your Data
Open browser console (F12) and type:
```javascript
JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_users'))      // All users
JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_cart'))       // Shopping cart
JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_orders'))     // Your orders
```
