# 🚀 QUICK START GUIDE - AK Industry E-Commerce

## Installation (Takes 30 seconds!)

1. **Open the project folder** - All files should be in one location
2. **Double-click `Index.html`** - Browser opens automatically
3. **Done!** Your e-commerce site is running! 🎉

---

## First Time Setup

### Step 1: Create Account
- Click **"Hello, sign in"** (top right)
- Click **"Register"** tab
- Fill in your details
- Password must be at least 6 characters
- Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Browse Products
- Scroll down to see product categories
- Use **search bar** to find products
- Click any product to see details

### Step 3: Add to Cart
- Click on a product
- Select quantity
- Click **"Add to Cart"**
- Green notification shows confirmation

### Step 4: Checkout
- Click **cart icon** (top right)
- Review items
- Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
- Fill delivery address
- Enter payment details (any card works)
- Click **"Place Order"**
- See order confirmation!

## Troubleshooting

### **Issue: Cart not showing items**
- **Fix:** Refresh page with `Ctrl+Shift+R`
- **Or:** Clear data: Open F12 console, type: `localStorage.clear()`, press Enter

### **Issue: Search not working**
- **Fix:** Make sure you're logged in
- **Or:** Try a different search term

### **Issue: Can't log in**
- **Fix:** Create a new account instead
- **Or:** Clear browser cache/cookies

### **Issue: Layout looks weird**
- **Fix:** Maximize browser window
- **Or:** Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

## Hidden Features 🎁

### View All Users
- Open browser console (F12)
- Type: `JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_users'))`
- Press Enter to see all registered users

### View Shopping Cart Data
- Type: `JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_cart'))`
- See all items in cart

### View Your Orders
- Type: `JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('ak_orders'))`
- See all your past orders

### Clear Everything
- Type: `localStorage.clear()`
- Clears all data (start fresh!)
